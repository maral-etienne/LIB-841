About My Life

Education

I graduated from the University of Louisville with a Bachelor of Science. Then, I went on to receive my Master of Arts in Education from Spalding University. I returned to Spalding to earn my Rank I, with certifications in Reading and ESL. Now, I am attending Eastern Kentucky University to obtain my Library Media Certification.

Work

I have worked with the Jefferson County Public School System since August of 2013 and have thoroughly enjoyed all the different roles I have experienced as a teacher.

Family

I have been married to my husband for 13 years now, and we have two beautiful children who keep me way too busy!

Hobbies

When I have time in between being a chauffeur for my children, I like to be outdoors—whether that's gardening, hiking, running, or playing with my children. When I am not outdoors, I enjoy building things. I have made a live-edge table with epoxy, built shelves and a desk for my children's room, and so much more.
