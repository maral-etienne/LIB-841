About This Website

Hello and welcome! This website documents my journey through the Library Media Certification program. My goal is to share this experience to inspire other adults to chase their dreams and not let age define what they can accomplish.
Thank you for taking the time to go on this journey with me!

While life can feel incredibly busy with work, family, and kids, it is so important to carve out space for the things that bring us true joy. Sometimes, that means intentionally stepping back from other demands to invest in our own happiness and personal growth.

Please feel free to look around. You can read more about my journey, challenges and success by navigating through the sections of this site.
